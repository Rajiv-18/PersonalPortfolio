About

Hello, I’m Rajiv Lomada. I’m a Software Engineering graduate from Ontario Tech University based in Toronto, with over 2 years of experience developing and delivering Salesforce solutions across enterprise-scale CRM environments.

My experience includes working across the full Salesforce development stack, building and deploying solutions using Apex, Lightning Web Components (LWC), SOQL, Flows, and REST/SOAP integrations. I hold three Salesforce certifications, including Salesforce Certified Agentforce Specialist, reflecting hands-on experience designing AI-driven agent workflows and intelligent automation solutions on the Salesforce platform.

I’m passionate about software engineering, cloud technologies, artificial intelligence, and digital transformation. I enjoy solving complex technical challenges, learning new technologies, and building reliable solutions that deliver meaningful business value.

Beyond my core Salesforce work, I have developed several full-stack applications, including HomeViewAI, an AI-powered real estate platform that combines computer vision, large language models, and semantic video search to create interactive property exploration experiences. I have also built a distributed IoT Smart Parking System and RehCreation, a live e-commerce platform focused on custom apparel and handcrafted products.

My technical interests include Salesforce development, CRM automation, cloud architecture, AI-powered applications, and full-stack software engineering. I enjoy combining platform technologies with modern software development practices to create scalable, user-focused solutions.
